How it Works & Details

How it Works

  • HolidayCoin can be purchased from Dec 25-31, 2017.
  • Purchases / donations can be made with Credit Card, Bitcoin, or Ether.
  • Upon purchasing, please choose a charity that you wish to receive the funds.
  • The funds are asymetrically distributed, with charities receiving the top 3 number of votes receiving a disproportionate amount of the funds according to the following distribution.
    1. First place receives 30% of the funds.
    2. Second place receives 15% of the funds.
    3. Third place receives 10% of the funds.
    4. The remaining 45% is shared equally amongst the other charities.
  • In order to help the mainstream adoption for cryptocurrency, we encourage the charities to receive their funds in Ether. Upon receipt of their address, we will transmit the funds to them. If they are unable or unwilling to receive the funds in Ether, we will covert the funds to their fiat currency of choice and send them via a check or wire.
  • Should a charity decline receipt of the funds, we will select a charity with a similar mission and goals.

Financial Transparcy

  • We will pass on all funds raised to the charities listed less our hard costs and a 2% administrative fee. This fee partially covers our time of running this project, including software development and marketing.
  • Our primary variable cost is credit card fees. We use Stripe to process credit cards. They charge a 2.9% + USD $0.30 fee per transaction.
  • To date we have spent about USD $1,750 to bootstrap this project, including graphic design (via contractors), domain registration, and marketing.
  • Possible future costs include but are not limited to exchange fees (between USD, EUD, BTC, and / or ETH), legal fees, accounting fees, statutory filing fees.
  • We plan to release 1/2 of the funds to the charities as soon as the fundraising is complete. We will hold the remaining funds for no more than 1 year to cover any unexpected costs which may occur. We our primarily converned with any unexpected legal, or accounting costs which we may need to cover. We will release the remaining funds to charities no later than Dec 31, 2018. We will release these funds earlier should we be assured that all material expenses have been covered.
  • All funds received are stored in Ethereum until they are distributed to the charities.
  • We are in the process of setting up a US-based 501(c)(3) (aka non-profit) organization. However, if the IRS does not approve our request then donations will not be tax deductible.
